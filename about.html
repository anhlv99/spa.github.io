About Us

About SPA
KingSpa

About SPA, located in the Sport Lisboa e Benfica Stadium, is an exclusive and innovative concept in the Fitness SPA universe.

Our chosen space allows our client to be involved in a pleasant feeling of comfort, tranquility and harmony. You will find at your disposal 3 individual treatment rooms, and a double room for couples therapies. We have 1 relaxation room where you can enjoy our delicious selection of teas. To complement your SPA treatment, we invite you to relax in the Thermogarden area of ​​the Life Club Academy, equipped with a dynamic pool, with 12 types of jets, sauna and Turkish bath(by appointment).



The About SPA team is made up of multifaceted elements, with vast experience in a wide range of areas related to Health and Well-Being. Specialists in excellent service, their main competences are rehabilitation and therapeutic massage, advanced aesthetics, aparatology, holistic therapies, manual lymphatic drainage and signature protocols, 100% adapted to the needs of each client.

Opening hours:
Monday to Friday: 10 am to 7 pm.
Saturday: 9am to 4pm.
Sunday: closed.
Holidays: 9am to 4pm.
(If there is SLB game at home, we are subject to change the closing time of the SPA)

KingSpa

At the KingSpa, our passion is to create a peaceful atmosphere, a place where you can reconnect with those who are most important to you, celebrate milestones, or simply pamper yourself. Delight in the ambience and enjoy our luxurious spa therapies dedicated to relaxing the mind, nurturing the body and uplifting the spirit. Especially designed with your needs in mind, we seek to enhance your levels of relaxation and well-being. Our specialized treatments include massage, facials, body wraps, body polishes, hydrotherapy baths, salon services and our amazing combination packages. Slip away for an hour or two, or spend the whole day with us and experience our unique selection of professional treatments and facilities.
