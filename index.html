KingSpa

At the KingSpa, our passion is to create a peaceful atmosphere, a place where you can reconnect with those who are most important to you, celebrate milestones, or simply pamper yourself. Delight in the ambience and enjoy our luxurious spa therapies dedicated to relaxing the mind, nurturing the body and uplifting the spirit. Especially designed with your needs in mind, we seek to enhance your levels of relaxation and well-being. Our specialized treatments include massage, facials, body wraps, body polishes, hydrotherapy baths, salon services and our amazing combination packages. Slip away for an hour or two, or spend the whole day with us and experience our unique selection of professional treatments and facilities.
